Prerequisites

Relax!, not much is needed from your side.
However, for a better understanding and ease of calculation,the basics of Machine Design and some vocabulary of Transmission would benefit you.

So, Are you Ready!!!!!!!!!!

Learnings

  • Basics of Transimssion

  • Greater details about CVT design parameters, optimisation, calculations, tuning and CVT cooling.

  • GearBox design, calculations ,heat dissipation and lubrication.

Recommended for

  • Automobile Enthusiasts irrespective of branch

  • Mechanical, Automotive ,Mechatronics & Production Engineering students.

  • Hightly recomended for new BAJA and FORMULA teams


        This workshop will set a strong foundation, enabling you to look forward to the recent R&D and the the latest technologies arriving in the field of Transmission
